# PulseFrame Commercial Licensing

PulseFrame is source-available and free for personal, non-commercial, and
internal business use under the terms in [LICENSE](LICENSE).

A paid commercial license is required if you want to:

- redistribute PulseFrame;
- resell PulseFrame;
- sublicense PulseFrame;
- offer PulseFrame as a hosted, managed, or SaaS service;
- embed PulseFrame into another product, platform, appliance, bundle, or
  paid service;
- provide PulseFrame to third parties as part of a commercial offering.

If you only want to run PulseFrame internally to monitor infrastructure
your own organization owns, leases, or controls, you do **not** need a
commercial license — see [LICENSE](LICENSE) Section 1(c) for what counts
as internal business use.

## How commercial licensing works

Commercial licenses are granted only under a separate written agreement
with Vimsoft. A commercial license grants only the rights expressly stated
in that agreement. No redistribution, resale, sublicensing, managed
hosting, SaaS, or embedding rights are granted unless the written agreement
explicitly allows them.

Pricing and terms depend on your intended use (e.g. redistribution volume,
number of end customers, SaaS vs. on-premise embedding), so there is no
fixed price list — contact us with a description of what you want to do.
